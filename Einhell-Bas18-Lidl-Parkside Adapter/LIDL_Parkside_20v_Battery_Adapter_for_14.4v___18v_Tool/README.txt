# Summary

General use adapter for LIDL 20v batteries for use with 14,4v and 18v tools.  The adapter is shown attached to the base of a 14.4v Ryobi drill after the Nicad power pack would no longer charge.  I have used this drill to screw down an MDF floor covering and the drill showed no signs of over heating after hundreds of screws.
Could easily be adapted to many other battery powered tools.
