## Print settings

* **White PLA** works perfectly but i think you can try other light colors or other materials (you will send me a photo :D)
* **0.15 layer height** works well for the Lithophane, and **0.3** works very well for the lamp support.
* Print the lithophane with **100%** infill, **10%** for the lamp support and **33%** or more for the lampshad support
* Don't print with a very high speed ;), not over 45 mm/s for the lithophane

Lithophane print time : **~20h**
Lamp support print time : **~4h50 with** 0.3 layer height and ~7h with 0.2
Lampshade support : **45 min** to 1h
